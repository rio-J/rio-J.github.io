Privacy Policy

We do not collect nor store any personal information about our users whatsoever. We do, however, make use of third party services which collect and store your data.

Third Party Services

DurakOne works with Google AdMob and Google Analytics, which help with advertising and with analyzing how users play the game. This allows us to keep DurakOne free for all to play. These services may collect and use personally identifiable information, including, but not limited to, advertising identifiers, geolocation, age group, gender, and internet protocol (IP) addresses. These same services may collect non personally identifiable information about the user, including, but not limited to, device type, operating system, screen resolution, and language.
